JVPA Clause 30.3 — Poor ESG Target Designation Framework
What is clause 30.3?

Clause 30.3 of the Joint Venture Participation Agreement establishes the mandatory process the Foundation must follow before acquiring shares in any company designated as a Poor ESG Target. The process is entrenched — it cannot be bypassed by the Caretaker Trustee or any governance body without member and FNAC consent.

The First Nations Advisory Committee (FNAC) is constituted under the JVPA. Its members are nominated by the Foundation's First Nations governance partners. No acquisition mandate may be issued until the FNAC has completed its review.

Why publish this?

Pre-publication is a structural accountability measure. Members and observers can verify every milestone as it is reached.

What happens at the end?

Once all seven milestones are complete, the Foundation is authorised to issue a first acquisition mandate for that company.
