Partnership notice

Your $4 partnership contribution establishes your Partner record, your Foundation S-NFT blockchain reservation record, and your Independence Vault. It is not a payment for a financial product.

The CJVM Hybrid Trust operates as a community joint venture partnership. The Foundation's self-assessed position is that the CJVM does not constitute a managed investment scheme under section 9 of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth), because Partners exercise day-to-day control over the trust's operations through their proprietary governance system. If applicable law later requires a specific authorisation or disclosure for a particular activity, the Trustee will obtain or provide what is required.

The Joint Venture Partnership Agreement Version 1.0 is the supreme governing instrument. By proceeding, you accept that Agreement and the entrenched founding principles of the CJVM Hybrid Trust.

▶ Key rights and obligations — summary disclosure (JVPA cl.8.1A)
What you are entitled to as a Partner
  • ◈ One equal governance vote on all Foundation-wide decisions — wealth does not change this (JVPA cl.1.5(d))
  • ◈ One Sub-Trust B income unit — proportional dividend participation once lawfully distributable (cl.1.5(n))
  • ◈ Your Independence Vault Wallet — operated by you autonomously, without Trustee involvement (cl.4.1A)
  • ◈ The right to submit governance proposals, cast votes, and participate in Partners Polls (cl.4.1A(b))
  • ◈ The right to nominate an heir — the sole exception to the non-transfer rule (cl.8.4)
  • ◈ The right to remove the Trustee by 75% Special Resolution at any time (cl.1.5(y))
What you are committing to as a Partner (JVPA cl.4.11)
  • ◈ Access the System at least once in any rolling 12-month period
  • ◈ Engage with Partners Polls and governance proposals to the extent reasonably practicable
  • ◈ Contribute to monitoring of the Partners Asset Pool through the System
  • ◈ Comply with the operational rules of the Joint Venture as set by Partners
Entrenched rules that protect you — cannot be changed by any resolution (JVPA cl.1.5)
  • ✓ $4 entry contribution is permanently fixed — no resolution may increase it
  • ✓ No COG$ Token may be sold for fiat currency or listed on any exchange
  • ✓ One Partner, one vote — governance cannot be purchased
  • ✓ Anti-capture cap: no person or entity may hold more than 1,000,000 Units across all classes
  • ✓ Sub-Trust B income distributions are mandatory and non-discretionary — within 60 days of receipt
  • ✓ Partners Asset Pool cannot be sold or distributed to private persons
  • ✓ First Nations Advisory Council cannot be removed or sidelined without 75% Special Resolution
  • ✓ Minimum 30% of Sub-Trust C distributions to First Nations programs every financial year
What this is not
  • — This is not an investment in a managed fund
  • — Your $4 partnership contribution is not refundable — it funds your permanent Partner record
  • — COG$ Tokens cannot be redeemed for cash under any circumstances

This is a summary only. The full agreement is the Joint Venture Partnership Agreement Version 1.0 (PDF). In the event of any inconsistency between this summary and the Agreement, the Agreement prevails.
