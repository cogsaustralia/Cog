The COG$ token

Asset-backed. Non-fiat. Goods and services only.

COG$ tokens are backed by real CHESS-held ASX shares in Australian resource companies — held by the Foundation on behalf of Partners. They are not a cryptocurrency you can sell for dollars. They cannot be listed on any exchange. They cannot be redeemed for fiat currency under any circumstances. This is an entrenched rule that no resolution of Partners or the board can remove.

What COG$ can be used for is real goods and services — at participating businesses. That is what makes your business participation foundational. Without businesses that accept COG$, Partners have no real-world place to spend, and the community economy exists only on paper. Your business is where it becomes real.

How COG$ circulates

Partners receive income through their partnership and spend COG$ at participating businesses — keeping value circulating within and between communities rather than bypassing them.

Sub-Trust A — Partners Asset Pool The Foundation holds real CHESS-registered ASX shares in resource companies. Dividends flow to Partners and fund community projects.
Sub-Trust B — Partner participation Partners hold income units and governance votes. When they spend COG$ at your business, value circulates within the community rather than leaving it.
Sub-Trust C — Community projects Donation COG$ proceeds fund community programs, First Nations custodianship initiatives, and local stewardship projects.
