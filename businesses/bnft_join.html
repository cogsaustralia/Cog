Business B-NFT Partnership Notice — Founding Phase

This is a founding-phase registration. All token figures and values are proposals of intent only. No financial product is being offered. No entitlement, issuance, or financial claim carries current financial effect.

The CJVM Hybrid Trust operates as a community joint venture partnership. The Foundation's self-assessed position is that the CJVM does not constitute a managed investment scheme under section 9 of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth). If applicable law later requires a specific authorisation or disclosure for a particular activity, the Trustee will obtain or provide what is required.

The B-NFT is a soulbound non-fungible token permanently linked to your ABN. It carries limited governance rights — specifically one vote on the appointment, removal, or replacement of the Trustee, consistent with the Joint Venture Partnership Agreement. Business Partners participate in Sub-Trust B distributions once the distribution system is activated on or after Expansion Day.

The Responsible Person named above will be the sole authorised operator of this business vault. They must hold a valid Personal S-NFT Partner record. By registering, you accept the Joint Venture Partnership Agreement.
