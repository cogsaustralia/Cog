1 What is COG$

A community that owns its say on Country.

COG$ started in Drake Village, NSW — a gold country town where locals decided there was another way. Instead of value being extracted from the land and flowing elsewhere, what if the community held a registered stake and a legal voice in what happens?

That's what COG$ is. A community joint venture. Not a fund, not an app, not a promise. A real shareholding in an ASX-listed resource company — owned collectively by members, governed by members, one equal vote each.

3 What We Own

Real shares. Real say at the company table.

Members collectively hold CHESS-registered shares in ASX-listed resource companies. CHESS is the national securities register — the same one every stockbroker in Australia uses. These are real shares, not tokens or certificates.

We are building toward a 1% holding in Legacy Minerals Holdings Limited (ASX:LGM). At 1%, we can formally attend AGMs, lodge resolutions, and vote on company decisions affecting Country. Our founding principle: minerals beneath the ground may carry greater long-term value left where they are than extracted.

📊

Not a fund. Not a promise. A registered shareholding and a community member legal voice on extraction and valuation.
