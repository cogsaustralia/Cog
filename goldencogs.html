Some individuals, institutions, and organisations do not just support COG$ — they make it possible. The Golden COG$ is our highest recognition, awarded with profound gratitude to those who have given their time, resources, expertise, and belief to help build a better, community-owned future for Australia.

These are the people and organisations who turned an idea into a movement. We are genuinely, deeply grateful.

Golden COG$ — #001

Our first Golden COG$

It is with enormous pride and heartfelt gratitude that we award the very first Golden COG$ to an organisation whose values, vision, and mission align perfectly with everything COG$ stands for — community, stewardship, and a sustainable future for the land and its people.

Golden COG$ — #001 · The Ingenious
The Global Hemp Movement
theghm.myshopify.com

The Global Hemp Movement represents everything that is possible when a community of passionate, principled people decides to take the future of the land into their own hands. Long before COG$ existed as an idea, the GHM was already doing the quiet, determined work of reconnecting communities with the earth, its resources, and each other.

Hemp is not simply a crop — it is a statement. A statement that land can regenerate rather than be depleted. That resources can sustain rather than be extracted. That communities can benefit from the earth they live on, rather than watching that benefit flow elsewhere. These are precisely the values that COG$ was built to amplify and protect across the entirety of Australia's resource sector.

The Global Hemp Movement's belief in community-led stewardship, in sustainable resource management, and in giving people a genuine stake in the land they call home made them a natural ally from the very beginning. Their support — in time, in energy, in shared purpose — has been a genuine gift to the COG$ community.

We are honoured to name The Global Hemp Movement as our first Golden COG$. May this recognition be a small reflection of the enormous gratitude and respect we hold for everything they represent and everything they do.

Golden COG$ — #002

A quiet force behind the scenes

Some of the most important contributions to any movement are never seen publicly. They happen in kitchens, over cups of tea, in late-night conversations and patient, unhurried listening. This Golden COG$ is for one of those people.

✦
Anonymous
Golden COG$ — #002 · Individual
The Maid
Preferred to remain anonymous

There are people in every great endeavour whose names never appear on a page, whose faces never appear in a photograph, but without whom nothing would have moved forward. The Maid is one of those people.

She asked the questions nobody else thought to ask — relentlessly, patiently, and always at exactly the right moment. Not questions born of scepticism, but the kind that come from genuine curiosity and quiet intelligence: the questions that force an idea to become sharper, clearer, and more honest with itself.

And when the talking was done, she listened. Not with half an ear, but with full and generous attention — the kind of listening that makes the person being heard feel truly understood. That quality, rare and extraordinary in any person, was a gift freely given time and again to the COG$ Foundation and to those building it.

We are quietly, warmly, and permanently grateful. The COG$ community is better for her presence in it, even if most of our members will never know her name.
