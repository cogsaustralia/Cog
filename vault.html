About this Vault

Radical transparency

The COG$ Community Vault is public by design. Every reservation is counted. Every governance vote is tallied. No individual member's data is visible here — only the collective total. This is how community ownership works: transparent, accountable, and open to scrutiny.

The data on this page updates whenever a member registers, adjusts their reservation, or casts a governance vote via their Independence Vault. Votes are anonymous — only aggregated totals are recorded and displayed.
