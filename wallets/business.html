Beta Phase — System Test

Welcome to the Beta Phase

Your business is part of the founding community helping build and test the COG$ platform before Expansion Day. Everything here is a live system test — your feedback shapes how this works for all Australian businesses.

✓ NO FINANCIAL OBLIGATION

All COG$ shown on this page are reservation-only. No payment is required. No financial commitment is made. These figures represent your intended interest only — nothing is offered, issued, or owed until Expansion Day when the Partners' governance system activates on-chain.

✎ CHANGE YOUR AMOUNTS ANYTIME

Your reserved COG$ amounts can be freely edited at any time using the controls on this page. Increase, decrease, or zero out any class — changes are recorded immediately with no penalty or cost.

⇄ TRANSFER TO MEMBERS & BUSINESSES

Use the Transfer tab to send ASX or RWA COG$ to any registered Partner or business. Each entity has a unique wallet address. Transfers are immediate and recorded on the ledger.

◈ WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

On Expansion Day, all reservation-only COG$ will be formalised under the Partners' on-chain governance system. Until then, your business governance participation rights are fully active.

Transfer rules

Which classes can be transferred?
ASX Investment COG$ and RWA COG$ are transferable. The Business B-NFT is permanently assigned and cannot be transferred.

Each business has a unique blockchain-style address per token class. Share your Receive address with senders. The address is cryptographically derived and cannot be reversed to your ABN.
No fiat conversion
No COG$ class permits fiat sale, redemption, or conversion. Members may agree their own non-fiat exchange value.
