Your vault — your partnership.

This is your Independence Vault — your operational interface to the CJVM Joint Venture. Your S-NFT Partner token is your key. Everything here belongs to you and your Partners collectively. The Trustee holds the legal safe. You and every other Partner run what is inside it.

Your Partner token.

Your Personal S-NFT COG$ gives you one equal vote on all Partnership-wide decisions. No Partner has more votes than you — not based on wealth, location, or seniority. Your token is soulbound — permanently yours, it cannot be sold or transferred.

The Partners Asset Pool.

Sub-Trust A holds CHESS-registered ASX shares directly in the Foundation's name — no intermediaries, no fund manager. You and your Partners direct how those shares are voted at company AGMs. The pool is permanent and non-disposable.

Partners Polls — how decisions are made.

Any 10 Partners, or 1% of all active Partners (whichever is fewer), may initiate a Partners Poll. Every Partner deliberates and votes. Ordinary decisions need a simple majority. Major decisions — Trustee appointment, JVPA amendments — require 75% agreement. The Trustee must implement what Partners vote for.

Dividends & Sub-Trust B.

50% of all Partners Asset Pool dividends flow to Sub-Trust B and are distributed proportionally to every Partner within 60 days of receipt. Sub-Trust C receives $2 from every Donation COG$ plus a share of its own dividend stream, directed toward community and Country projects.
